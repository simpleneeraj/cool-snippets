# Privacy Policy for Crystal Codium

Welcome to Crystal Codium! Your privacy and trust are very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app and services.

## Who We Are

Crystal Codium is the ultimate solution for effortlessly generating and sharing code snippets. Our app helps you create, customize, and share code snippets in various programming languages. Boost your productivity, collaborate seamlessly on social media, and join a thriving community of developers. Try Crystal Codium today!

## Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

### 1. Account Information

To use Crystal Codium, you can log in through:

- **Google**
- **GitHub**
- **Email Sign-Up**

When you sign up or log in, we may collect:

- Your name
- Email address
- Profile picture (if available)

### 2. Public Code Snippets

If you choose to share code snippets publicly, they may be visible to other users and used to help the developer community. By sharing publicly, you give us permission to feature or share these snippets to benefit others.

### 3. Usage Data

We may collect information about how you use our app, such as:

- Features you interact with
- Frequency and duration of use
- Device type and browser details

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide and improve our services
- Personalize your experience in the app
- Facilitate login and account management
- Build a helpful developer community

## Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share information:

- With trusted third-party services for authentication (Google and GitHub)
- If required by law or to protect our rights

## Your Choices

- You can control the visibility of your code snippets (public or private).
- You can update or delete your account information at any time.

## Security

We take security seriously and use modern techniques to protect your data. However, no system is entirely secure, so please use our app responsibly.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed to reflect changes in our practices. We’ll notify you of significant changes through the app or email.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at **support@crystalcodium.com**.

Thank you for trusting Crystal Codium! Together, let’s create a better coding experience for everyone.
