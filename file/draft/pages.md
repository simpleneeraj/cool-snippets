2. **Contact**

   - Contact form for user queries or feedback (Name, Email, Message).

3. **About Us**

   - Brief history and mission of Crystal Codium.
   - Introduction to the team behind the application.
   - Vision and roadmap for the future.

4. **Pricing**

   - Detailed comparison of Free, Pro, and Premium plans.
   - Features available for each plan.
   - Call-to-action for plan subscription.

5. **Terms and Conditions | Privacy Policy**
   - Terms outlining the usage policies and guidelines.

   - Separate sections or collapsible panels for easier navigation.
