### Sidebar Components

#### Primary Panels

- `PrimaryPanel`: Main content area.
- `SecondaryPanel`: Additional or contextual information.

#### Drawers

- `MainDrawer`: Main navigation or content.
- `AuxiliaryDrawer`: Additional tools or options.

#### Containers

- `ContentContainer`: Primary content.
- `ContextContainer`: Context-specific information.

#### Sidebars

- `AppSidebar`: General app navigation.
- `InfoSidebar`: Supplementary information.
- `BottomSidebar`: Content at the bottom.

#### Panels

- `PanelA`, `PanelB`: Generic names for interchangeable or context-dependent content.

#### Sections

- `Section1`, `Section2`: Sections for organizing content.

#### Navigation and Enhancement

- `Navigator`: Guiding or navigating.
- `Enhancer`: Additional tools or features.

#### Master and Detail Panels

- `MasterPanel`: Primary area.
- `DetailPanel`: Secondary or detailed information.

#### Bottom Segment

- `BottomSegment`: Content at the bottom.
